Team Ichor Perfusion Simulator System

A team consisting of two software engineering and two mechanical engineering students was tasked with producing a complete perfusion simulation system for the perfusion department at Quinnipiac University's Frank D. Netter School of Medicine.

The perfusion simulator system contains two main subsystems: a mechanical device to sequester water and a software system to control the mechanical device and to allow the student to practice real perfusion scenarios on an iPad.
